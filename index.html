Business Law

Owning a business isn’t easy. You strive to maintain a positive team atmosphere and excellent client experience, but any successful leader knows that you cannot ignore the legal and financial side of the business. Unfortunately, those who do leave the legal end of their business undone are often shocked when hit with complaints or lawsuits.

Fortunately, it is never too late to get your business legally sound. With the team at Gandhi Selim Law, you can develop systems that will protect yourself, your team, and your business. You just have to make the first step – calling us to set up a consultation.

Our team has worked with businesses of all sizes. We’ve worked with for-profits and not-for-profits, as well as private and public companies. In our time working with business leaders, we’ve learned that most simply don’t know what they need to do to protect their business. That’s where we come in.

We will listen to the story of your business, keeping our eyes trained on what you have done in the past and where you want the business to go in the future. With this information, we will work side by side with you to write legally sound contracts or even restructure your business plan. We can be there to create new branches or transfer property from one party to the other. Whatever you need to reach your goals, our team can guide you through the process and offer counsel to bring to your Board, your employees, or your clients.

Most importantly, we protect you. We specialize in drafting contracts that assure that protect your personal assets, assuring that third parties, employees or partners cannot pierce the corporate veil. We believe that your personal assets should never be subject to a lawsuit, and protect you as such.

Don’t put off the legal responsibilities of owning or leading a business. Instead, allow our team to take the lead on some business law initiatives that will keep your business more viable in the future.

Give us a call to set up a free 30 minute consultation today; we love meeting with business owners and sharing our experience, while learning from you as well.
